“There’s a portion of us that exists that cannot be seen directly, but it can be spoken to.”

The Dissent: Greatness among Us is a volume of poetry, personal stories, and reflective prose that shares insights from one man’s journey through life as he learns valuable lessons, leans on his faith in God, and discovers that we are all more alike than not.

"This is for all.
We must focus on gaining more insight to who we are as individuals."

In a collection of poems, anecdotes, and reflective prose based on real experiences and inspired by his faith in Jesus Christ, Isaiah White shares valuable truths while inviting others to focus on what is important, reflect on the world around them, and see life in a different light. While exploring his own journey, White traverses through the waters of “what it seems” to provide inspiration to others seeking their own truths and a new path forward.

"Our boats float the same, though there are different strokes."

This book is a written account of my time meditating about the things that I have read and witnessed through the scope of my faith in Jesus Christ. I aim to encourage an open mind and heart for every reader to provoke a new outlook in life. This book will not align with any one outlook. This is my exploration of Truth, traversing through the waters of “what it seems.”

About Isaiah

Isaiah is a Texas native that believes a person's upbringing does not have to conflict with the differences of others, but can complement those differences to push society closer to the truth of life.
