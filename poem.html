The Dissent

A collection of Poetry from the Author

Are You Available

Part of the mass, now singled to your own piece
Stood with the group, but now you’re on your own feet
There was a time when no one seemed to leave you
No it seems, no one tends to see you
No longer identified with the world’s people
If they mistreat you, they did not commit a crime
Or violate your rights, because you’re not seen as equal
And you’re not, because of what’s in the inside
It’s like a star, because it heats as it shines
But it doesn’t need a stage to bring attention to it’s light
You won’t blaze for the moment, and burnout by the morning
But wax for the torn, and shed for the mourning
This isn’t ordinary, to most intimidating
That you of no value exceeded expectations
If they won’t detain you, self will restrain you
But none can stop the truth, if you’re available

All have the pine in their eyes to be great
But can you lionize the lives soaked in strife
To toss a lifeline in the seas made of their crying
Be a spark to the flame of the soul for their drying
Most of us just aspire
As long as we keep eating, other’s lives can expire
But I feel we come to a choice in our lives
To be glutenous in self, or stop eating the plate of lies
Fasting from self, to try and fasten yourself
To a diet that is fashioned for health
Cease from living through appetites, to ample life
Beyond the desires to postpone death/
Claiming you’re living to the fullest, sounds foolish
When nothing trickles down during your constant moving
Leaving the vessels under you half empty and destitute
To the brim you weren’t filled, cause space wasn’t available

More to Life

There’s more to life than what we see right before us.
Getting caught in “could’s” and “would’s”, it’ll get as thick as a forest.
There’s more to life than the ability to touch.
Because once that thing is gone, you won’t feel much.
There’s more to life than things that are satisfying.
As long as you have eyes, you won’t fill the voids in your iris.
There’s more to life than bodies that attract.
If it doesn’t know love, then it won’t love back.
Which means there’s more to life than knowing one another.
If I don’t know me, how could I know another.
But there’s more to life than the existence of your views.
Wisdom is not gained from disclosing your own truth.
And, there’s more to life than the life of your struggles.
All have strife, and none is worse than another’s.
Not to blind you, just trying to shed light
On the truth, that there’s more to life.

There’s more to life than all the worlds cash.
It can’t buy love, peace, and grace that’ll last.
There’s more to life than women that are fast.
Because at the right speed, you’re sure to lose track.
There’s more to life than having a lot of stones.
Possibly all the ice may make one emotionless.
So, there’s more to life than being the coolest.
Because the hottest trends are growing cold.
There’s more to life than trying to please people.
If you favor one, the others feel unequal.
So, there’s more to life than what they say.
Keep your eyes focused and follow what’s been paved.
There’s more to life than the world’s deafness.
You know you heard a word, they don’t want you to listen.
They may frown because you shine too bright,
But show them that there’s more to life.

Loiterer

I've been around here for a minute.
You thought I left, but I'm still up in this.
Real adversaries will always tarry
For your demise, if they are living.
I have some work that I haven't finished.
I am the hurt of you daily grimace.
You've been distracted, I've been relaxing
Because you believe there's bigger business.
Because you perceive there's better interest.
And you grieve in their disappearance.
But, their importance is a distortion
Of your life and the way you live it.
Maybe you can see me very vivid.
But, you are scared of my existence.
So, you cover up with the other stuff
To take your mind off your condition.
Trying to act like we're not conflicting,
  And I'm not really fucking with your feelings.
But to get rid of THIS, you must be intimate
With yourself and with the enemy.
Or, you too will become a villain.
  Viewing the world with a very cynic
Type of mindset, thinking you found it
Because you express your hurt in excess.
Believing a cold heart gave you access
To all the facts of Life's secrets.
  But, that's not possible without control.
See, your revelations are from my genesis.
Every step you took, I was the cause of it.
Everything you made, I was the elements.
I devoured all of the power
The very hour that you neglected me.
So, sweep me under the rug.
The dirt will soon pile up.
I know how big your closet is.
There's not enough room for the skeletons.
Because you share things in marriages,
And bondage is what this marriage is.
Don't you think that I'll just pack and leave.
I'll wear this ring until you get rid of me.
... So face me.

The Jewel

It’s the only thing I have to give.
It’s the only reason I rise to live.
It’s the greater part of my identity.
It’s because of this you notice me.
I was confused of its existence.
Thought it was something that needed seeking.
Thought it would be earned with my labor,
But it’s my work to share it with my neighbor.
Daily, it was perfected.
For the moments to share it, in discretion.
But Wisdom says give it unfiltered,
So that at my lowest others are lifted.
So, this is the last judgement.
A death sentence to future grudges.
The new law under the present verdict.
The best of me, regardless if you deserve it.

- Isaiah White
